# Code of Conduct

BonyanOSS contributors are expected to work with adab, clarity, and respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and precise.
- Assume good intent, but correct issues clearly.
- Keep technical feedback focused on the work.
- Welcome beginners and help them make useful contributions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Religious mockery or sectarian provocation.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or repeated low-effort disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, or restrict participation when needed to protect the project.
